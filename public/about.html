About Me

Hello! I'm Mai Tran, a South Florida native with a passion for turning ideas into digital reality. My journey into web development began in high school with an Intro to Web Design class, where I first discovered the magic of coding. That spark led me to 4Geeks Academy, where I leveled up my skills through their intensive coding bootcamp—proof that I thrive in fast-paced, challenging environments.

By day, I'm a Detail-Oriented Product Lister at a luxury resale boutique in Boca Raton. My role is equal parts detective and storyteller: I research, photograph, and meticulously document high-end items to create listings that bridge the gap between online shoppers and physical products. Juggling inventory, customer requests, and front-end support has sharpened my ability to prioritize, multitask, and problem-solve—skills that translate seamlessly into coding.
